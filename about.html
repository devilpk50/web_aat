Who We Are

Vastra Revival is a sustainable fashion marketplace where people can buy and sell pre-loved and recycled clothing. Our mission is to reduce textile waste while making stylish fashion affordable for everyone.

Every piece you see here helps save water, energy, and the environment.

Sustainability

Reducing fashion waste through reuse and recycling.

Community

Empowering people to sell and buy responsibly.

Impact

Small choices today create a greener tomorrow.
